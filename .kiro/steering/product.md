# Product — kiro-mind

## Purpose
Personal Obsidian vault serving as an external brain for an individual contributor.

## Core Use Cases
- Capture work notes, decisions, and context as they happen
- Track performance evidence and brag-worthy accomplishments
- Prepare for performance reviews and 1-1s with structured data
- Build a searchable graph of people, projects, and decisions
- Surface patterns across work over time

## Audience
Single individual contributor (IC). Not multi-user.

## Non-Goals
- **Not a team wiki** — no shared editing, no permissions model
- **Not a project management tool** — no kanban, no sprint tracking, no ticket system
- **Not a CRM** — people notes are context for *your* work, not relationship management
- **Not a second Slack** — no meeting transcripts dumped verbatim; distill first

## Design Principles
1. Low friction capture > perfect structure
2. Link liberally — the graph is the product
3. Evidence over opinion — attach artifacts to claims
4. Review-ready at any time — brag log and competency map stay current
5. Archive, don't delete — past context has future value
